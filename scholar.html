Available Scholarships

University of Alabama

  • Crimson Scholar Achiever

- A student with a 25 ACT or 1200-1230 SAT score and minimum 3.5 cumulative GPA will be selected as a Crimson Achievement Scholar and will receive $8,000 over four years ($2,000 per year).

  • UA Legends

- A student with a 26 ACT or 1240-1270 SAT score and minimum 3.5 cumulative GPA will be selected as a UA Legends Scholar and will receive $10,000 over four years ($2,500 per year).

  • President Elite Scholar

- A student with a 4.0 GPA and a 36 ACT or 1600 SAT will be selected as a Presidential Elite Scholar and will receive:

  1. Value of tuition for up to four years for degree-seeking undergraduate and graduate or law studies
  2. One year of on-campus housing at regular room rate
  3. $1,000 per year stipend for four years
  4. $2,000 one-time allowance for use in summer research or international study (after completing one year of study at UA)
  5. $2,000 book scholarship ($500 per year for four years)

University of Missouri

  • Diversity Award

- Requirements:

  • Competitive high school academic record, including grades, class rank and ACT or SAT scores
  • U.S. citizen or permanent resident
  • xEnrollment at MU the first semester after high school graduation
  • Membership in a racial or ethnic minority group that is underrepresented in relation to the University's goal to achieve educational benefits of a diverse student body
  • First preference given to Missouri residents
  • Students admitted on probation are not eligible for the Diversity Award

- Award:

  • Missouri resident: $2,500 per year
  • Non-Missouri resident: Waiver of nonresident tuition (approximately $15,000). Waiver of nonresident tuition is for a total of 8 regular semesters (fall and spring), students are not eligible for the waiver during summer terms
  • Excellence Award

- Requirements:

  • Top 50 percent of high school graduating class
  • Composite ACT score of 27 or higher (or an SAT score of 1280 or higher for the new exam/1210 or higher pre-March 2016 exam)
  • U.S. citizen or permanent resident
  • Missouri resident
  • Enrollment at MU the first semester after high school graduation

- Award Amount:

  • Top 25%: $2,000
  • Top 50%: $1,000
